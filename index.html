About Me

I graduated from the Hanyang University, Korea with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering (2021) and from Korea Advanced Institute of Science& Technology (KAIST) with a M.S. (2023). I am currently a Ph.D candidate in KAIST under the guidance of my supervisor professor Chang D. Yoo.

In my research, my focus in on enhancing reliability of AI and Deep Learning tools when working with images, videos, and languages. I really enjoy discovering more about what these tools can do, and I'm always looking for ways to make them work better and reliable in real-world situations. My ultimate goal is to contribute to the development of socially acceptable artificial intelligence through ongoing, in-depth research efforts.
